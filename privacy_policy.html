Privacy Policy for MyTasks App

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how the MyTasks App operates, which is an automated content scheduling and posting tool designed for business pages (like restaurants) and personal profiles. This policy is designed to comply with general best practices for handling user-generated and API-provided content.

1. Scope of Data Collection

The MyTasks App is designed to help users automate the creation and scheduling of social media content. We only collect data necessary to execute this function:

  • Input Topics: The core themes, keywords, or raw information provided by the user to initiate a content plan.
  • Generated Content: The final, processed text, and potential accompanying metadata (like source URLs or image descriptions) created by the AI engine.
  • System Metadata: Non-personal data required for system tracking, such as timestamps, task IDs, and task flow IDs.
  • Platform Data: Information passed through the Meta Graph API (e.g., successful post confirmations, error codes).

2. Use of Data & Service Purpose

The collected data is used *exclusively* for the following purposes:

  1. Content Generation: To brainstorm and refine high-quality, engaging content based on the provided topics.
  2. Scheduling: To structure and sequence posts according to a predetermined calendar.
  3. Posting (Live or Draft): To submit the final content to the designated Facebook/Instagram Page for publishing.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We rely on third-party services to make this service function:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. (Facebook/Instagram): We must send data to Meta's Graph API using your provided `PAGE_ACCESS_TOKEN` to publish content. We do not store or use this token for any purpose other than posting to the specified page.
  • AI Processing: Data is temporarily processed by the language model to generate content.

We guarantee: We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any entity for marketing or unrelated purposes.

4. Data Retention & Your Rights

Retention: We aim to retain only the minimum data necessary. Technical logs are kept for a limited period (e.g., 30 days) for debugging. The final generated content drafts are saved locally and returned to you, and you are responsible for their archival.

Your Rights: You have the right to request a copy of all data we have processed for you, or to request its deletion, by contacting us through the designated support channel.
